Our Partners & Collaborators

YES+ collaborates with a diverse range of organizations to provide the best opportunities for Tibetan youth. Our partnerships enable us to expand our reach, enhance our programs, and create meaningful impact in the community. We are grateful for the continued support and collaboration of these organizations.

USAID

The United States Agency for International Development (USAID) supports YES+ through funding for vocational training programs and entrepreneurship initiatives. This partnership has enabled us to expand our reach and provide more comprehensive support to Tibetan youth seeking to develop marketable skills.

Visit Website

The Tibet Fund

The Tibet Fund collaborates with YES+ on educational initiatives and scholarship programs for Tibetan youth. Together, we work to ensure that young Tibetans have access to quality education and vocational training, helping them build sustainable livelihoods while preserving their cultural heritage.

Visit Website

Social and Resource Development Fund (SARD)

SARD partners with YES+ to implement community development projects and social entrepreneurship initiatives. This collaboration focuses on sustainable development within Tibetan communities, providing youth with opportunities to contribute meaningfully to their communities while developing valuable skills.

Visit Website

Dalai Lama Institute for Higher Education (DLIHE)

YES+ collaborates with DLIHE to provide specialized vocational training programs that complement academic education. This partnership creates pathways for students to gain practical skills alongside their academic studies, enhancing their employability and career prospects.

Visit Website

Norzin Consultancy

Norzin Consultancy provides expertise in program development and evaluation for YES+ initiatives. Their consultative support helps us enhance the effectiveness of our programs, ensuring that they meet the evolving needs of Tibetan youth in the contemporary job market.

Visit Website

Javed Habib Hair & Beauty

YES+ partners with Javed Habib Hair & Beauty academy to offer professional hairdressing and beautician training. This collaboration provides Tibetan youth with opportunities to develop skills in the beauty industry, which has strong employment prospects both within the Tibetan community and the broader market.

Visit Website

Gu-Chu-Sum Tibetan Ex-Political Prisoners Movement

YES+ works with Gu-Chu-Sum to provide support and vocational training for former political prisoners and their families. This partnership focuses on rehabilitation and economic empowerment, helping individuals rebuild their lives through skill development and employment support.

Visit Website

Institute for Advanced Computer Technology (IACT)

IACT collaborates with YES+ to deliver specialized IT and digital skills training to Tibetan youth. This partnership equips young people with the technical skills needed in today's digital economy, opening up new opportunities in the technology sector.

Visit Website

Confederation of Indian Industry - Managed Career Center (CII-MCM)

YES+ partners with CII-MCM to connect trained Tibetan youth with employment opportunities in the Indian market. This collaboration includes job placement services, career counseling, and industry-specific training to enhance the employability of program participants.

Visit Website

Orbit Hospitality

Orbit Hospitality works with YES+ to provide hospitality training and internship opportunities for Tibetan youth. This partnership focuses on developing skills in hotel management, food service, and customer care, opening doors to careers in the growing hospitality sector.

Visit Website

TibetFreunde

TibetFreunde supports YES+ through funding for educational and vocational training programs. This German-based organization has been a long-standing partner, helping to expand educational opportunities for Tibetan youth in exile.

Visit Website

EKAA

EKAA collaborates with YES+ on cultural preservation initiatives that integrate with vocational training. This partnership focuses on developing skills that allow Tibetan youth to maintain their cultural heritage while building sustainable livelihoods.

Visit Website

Envision

Envision works with YES+ to provide entrepreneurship training and mentoring for Tibetan youth interested in starting their own businesses. This partnership focuses on developing business skills, providing startup support, and creating networks of young Tibetan entrepreneurs.

Visit Website
