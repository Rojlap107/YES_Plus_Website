Digital Skills Training

All Programmes > Digital Skills Training

Programme Overview

A hands-on training program focused on developing practical digital skills that enhance employability in today's job market. Courses cover web development, digital marketing, graphic design, and data analysis.

Programme Highlights

  • Industry-relevant curriculum designed with input from tech professionals
  • Hands-on projects and portfolio development
  • Job placement assistance and internship opportunities
  • One-on-one mentorship from industry experts
  • Networking events with potential employers
  • Post-program career support and continued learning resources

Learning Tracks

Participants can choose from the following specialization tracks:

  • Web Development - HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and responsive design
  • Digital Marketing - SEO, social media marketing, content creation, and analytics
  • Graphic Design - Adobe Creative Suite, UI/UX principles, and brand identity design
  • Data Analysis - Excel, SQL, basic Python, and data visualization

Programme Details

Duration: 12 weeks
Format: Hybrid (Online & In-person)
Eligibility: Tibetan youth aged 18-35
Batch Size: 25 participants per track
Next Batch: August 2025
Application Deadline: June 30, 2025

Course Structure

Phase 1: Core Skills (4 weeks)

All participants learn foundational digital skills, computer literacy, professional communication, and project management basics.

Phase 2: Specialization Track (6 weeks)

Intensive training in your chosen specialization with practical projects and skill development.

Phase 3: Capstone Project & Job Placement (2 weeks)

Complete a portfolio-quality project, refine your resume, practice interview skills, and connect with potential employers.
